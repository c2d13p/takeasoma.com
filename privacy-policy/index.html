The site uses fonts loaded from Google Fonts, which may collect technical data (e.g., IP address) during connection to their servers. For more information, see the Google Fonts privacy policy.

The processing of technical and anonymous data is carried out under Article 6, paragraph 1, letter f of the GDPR, for purposes related to the legitimate interest of the data controller in providing a functional website and collecting anonymous statistics. The processing of data for blog subscriptions is based on the explicit consent of the user.

User Rights
